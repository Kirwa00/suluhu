Q1: How should a clinician prioritize a patient reporting passive suicidal ideation with no immediate plan?

check_circle

Therapist's Answer

Schedule a standard follow-up within 48 hours and complete a safety plan.

Correct Answer

Schedule a standard follow-up within 48 hours and complete a safety plan.

Clinical Rationale

"While ideation without plan is lower risk than active ideation, Suluhu's protocol mandates a formal safety plan within 48 hours to mitigate escalation risk. This balances safety with appropriate level of care."

Q7: A client mentions a sudden onset of severe physical symptoms during a trauma-focused session. What is the immediate action?

cancel

Therapist's Answer

Utilize grounding techniques and proceed with the session if symptoms stabilize.

Correct Answer

Pause session immediately and assess for medical emergency (Triage Step 1).

Clinical Rationale

"In cases of sudden physical distress, medical stabilization must take precedence over psychological processing to ensure physiological safety before returning to therapeutic work."
